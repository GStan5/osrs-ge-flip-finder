GE flipping basics

Flipping is buying items on the Grand Exchange below instant sell price, then selling above your buy — after 2% GE tax (capped at 5M per item).

Workflow on Graardor

  1. Open Flip Finder and refresh prices when starting a session.
  2. Sort by GP/hour or margin — check est. buy/sell time vs your patience.
  3. Click prices to copy; place offers in-game and wait.
  4. Log completed flips in the flip log.
